Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose, also known as Netaji, was a fearless Indian nationalist who played a crucial role in the struggle for India's independence from British colonial rule. He was born on January 23, 1897, in Cuttack, Odisha, India.

Netaji Bose is renowned for his leadership in the Indian National Army (INA) and his famous slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom." His dedication to the cause of independence and his unwavering spirit continue to inspire generations.
